# Chapter 1: Introduction

## 1.1 Project Overview
The **Document Tracking System (DTS)** is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to streamline and automate the management of organizational documents. In modern administrative environments, the sheer volume of documents—ranging from official memos to internal reports—requires a robust tracking mechanism to ensure accountability, prevent loss, and expedite approval processes.

## 1.2 Problem Statement
Traditional document handling often suffers from several critical issues:
- **Lack of Transparency:** It is often difficult to determine the current location or status of a physical or emailed document.
- **Manual Delays:** Approval workflows that rely on physical movement or unmanaged email threads are prone to bottlenecks.
- **Risk of Loss:** Important documents can be misplaced or forgotten without a centralized registry.
- **Audit Challenges:** Retracing the history of a document’s journey for audit purposes is time-consuming and often incomplete.

## 1.3 Solution Overview
The DTS addresses these challenges by providing a centralized digital repository where every document is assigned a unique identity. The system provides:
- **Real-time Tracking:** See exactly where a document is in its lifecycle.
- **Automated Workflows:** Route documents through pre-defined or dynamic organizational steps.
- **Secure Storage:** Centralized, backed-up storage for all document files.
- **Comprehensive Audit Logs:** Automatic recording of every action taken on a document.

## 1.4 Goals and Objectives
The primary objectives of the Document Tracking System are:
1.  **Efficiency:** To reduce the time spent on document processing and approvals.
2.  **Accountability:** To provide a clear record of responsibility for every action taken.
3.  **Security:** To ensure that documents are only accessible to authorized personnel and departments.
4.  **Accessibility:** To provide users with easy access to the documents they need, whenever they need them.

## 1.5 Scope of the System
The DTS is designed for use within institutional or corporate environments. Its scope includes:
- Document creation, uploading, and categorization.
- Multi-step routing between different departments and users.
- Notification alerts for pending actions.
- Archiving and historical retrieval of documents.
- Detailed reporting and analytics on document flow.
